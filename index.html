About Game Forge

Game Forge is a dynamic gaming club dedicated to game development and competitive gaming, forging the future of esports and innovation. It provides students with hands-on projects, industry exposure, and networking opportunities to develop skills, collaborate, and explore careers in gaming.

Vision

Game Forge aims to raise awareness of the gaming industry, offering students opportunities to compete, create, and connect. Through tournaments, hackathons, and industry visits, the club fosters skill development, esports culture, and career pathways in game development, content creation, and streaming.

Objectives

  • Promote gaming as a career and industry
  • Host workshops, competitions, and networking events
  • Provide hands-on experience in game development and esports
  • Encourage responsible gaming and ethical esports
